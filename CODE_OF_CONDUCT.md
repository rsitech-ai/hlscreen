# Code Of Conduct

`hlscreen` is a technical project for public market-data tooling. Discussion should stay professional, concrete, and evidence-based.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful and direct.
- Critique code, claims, assumptions, and evidence rather than people.
- Keep trading and risk claims precise; do not hype results or imply guarantees.
- Protect private data, credentials, and account information.
- Help maintain a project where newcomers can reproduce results and understand decisions.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing secrets, private keys, account data, or private endpoints.
- Encouraging live trading, order execution, or financial decisions through this read-only project.
- Repeatedly derailing technical review with unsupported claims.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations can lead to blocking from the project.

To report conduct issues, contact the maintainer privately through GitHub.
